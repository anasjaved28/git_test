Description

A quick and simple Italian pasta recipe made with garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic (thinly sliced)
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Drain and save 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sautÃ© garlic until golden.
  3. Add chili flakes and stir for a few seconds.
  4. Toss in the cooked spaghetti and mix well.
  5. Add a splash of pasta water if it feels too dry.
  6. Season with salt, garnish with parsley, and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with garlic bread and a fresh salad. Top with parmesan if desired.
